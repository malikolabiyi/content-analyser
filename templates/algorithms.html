Headline

Algorithms used for the headline...

Word Balance

Power Word: Power words also known as phrases indicates intense trigger words that frequently command a reader's attention and action. It is recommended that a headline should contain at least 1 power phrase or word.

Common Word: Common words make up the basic structure of readable headlines. It is recommended that a headline should be made up of 20 - 30% common words.

Uncommon Word: Uncommon words occur less frequently than common words, but give your headline substance. It is recommended that a headline should be made up of 10-20% uncommon words.

Search Engine Optimisation

Word Length: The word length is also a crucial factor, especially for the Search Engine.

Keyword in Headline: Your headline(title) is what to be shown on the search engine, so your focus keyword's appearance is important so that people will know what your content is all about.

Number in Headline: Though, this isn't really recommended, but if you're writing a content that involves giving lists (for example: 9 ways to make money online), it is crucial to make use of numbers. Odd numbers are the best to use.
