Word Balance

Power Word: Power words also known as phrases indicates intense trigger words that frequently command a reader's attention and action. It is recommended that a headline should contain at least 1 power phrase or word.

Common Word: Common words make up the basic structure of readable headlines. It is recommended that a headline should be made up of 20 - 30% common words.

Uncommon Word: Uncommon words occur less frequently than common words, but give your headline substance. It is recommended that a headline should be made up of 10-20% uncommon words.
